## 💬 When You Feel Like Quitting — Read This

You will hit a wall. Probably around Day 20–30, again around Day 60, and again around Day 100.

At Day 20–30: You'll realize how much there is to learn. This feels overwhelming. It means you understand the scope now. That's good. Narrow your focus. Do the next single task.

At Day 60: You'll feel like you're learning but not building anything impressive. This is the valley. Push through. Phase 2 is right there.

At Day 100: You'll compare yourself to people posting on LinkedIn who seem much further ahead. Remember: they've been doing this for 2–5 years. You've been doing it for 3 months.

The people who become experts are not smarter. They are the ones who showed up on Day 300 when it was hard. That will be you.
