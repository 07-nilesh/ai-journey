## 💬 WHEN YOU FEEL LIKE QUITTING — READ THIS

You will hit a wall at Day 20–30, Day 60, Day 100, and Day 200.

**At Day 20–30:** You realize how much there is to learn. This feels overwhelming. It means you understand the scope now — that's progress, not a problem. Narrow your focus. Do the next single task.

**At Day 60:** You feel like you're learning but not building anything impressive. This is the valley every learner crosses. Phase 2 is right there. Push through.

**At Day 100:** You compare yourself to people on LinkedIn who look much further ahead. They've been doing this 2–5 years. You've been doing it 3 months. The comparison is invalid.

**At Day 200:** You wonder if you should switch direction, try a different specialization, or take a "better" course. This is resistance. Stay the course.

**The truth:** The people who become AI engineers and founders are not smarter than you. They are the people who showed up on Day 300 when it was hard. When it was boring. When progress felt invisible.

That will be you. Just keep showing up.
